the ionSpinner directive provides a variety of animated spinners.

Ionic offers ten spinners out of the box, and by default, it will use the appropriate spinner for the platform on which it's running

Use Platform Default

<ion-spinner></ion-spinner>

Selecting Spinner

<ion-spinner icon="bubbles"></ion-spinner>

Coloring Spinner

Spinners can also be styled using Ionic's standard color naming convention.

<ion-spinner
    icon="bubbles"
    class="spinner-energized">
